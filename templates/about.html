About Us

Genuine Local Florists - Helping You Express Your Feelings With Flowers!

Company Background

Established in 1968, Cupertino Florist has been a cornerstone in the Cupertino and San Jose communities, offering exceptional floral arrangements and gifts for over five decades.

As a family-owned and operated business, we pride ourselves on delivering only the finest floral creations, supported by friendly and prompt service.

“Happiness held is the seed; Happiness shared is the flower.”

— John Harrigan

Our offerings include a wide variety of fresh domestic and exotic flowers, plants, and custom arrangements suitable for all occasions. We also provide services for weddings, funerals, and other special events, ensuring that each arrangement is tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our store is located at 7289 Coronado Dr, San Jose, California, 95129, where we continue to serve our community with the same passion and dedication that has defined our business since its inception.
